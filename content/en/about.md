# About Istros Society

The Society of Friends of Cyprus and Greece in Slovakia is a non-profit organization.

## Mission

Our mission is to promote cultural and social relations between Slovakia, Cyprus, and Greece through educational programs, cultural events, and exchange activities.

## History

The organization was founded to strengthen friendly relations and cultural exchange between our nations.
